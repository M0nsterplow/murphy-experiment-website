About

The Murphy Experiment is a sonic laboratory where musical boundaries go to die. Born from the creative chaos of Tempe, Arizona in 2022, we blend progressive rock with humor, experimentation, and unapologetic originality into something uniquely unhinged.


Our sound careens from introspective melodies to full-throttle musical mayhem, always with a wink and a nod to the absurdity of it all.


When things get really out of hand, our sippy cup-beer drinking alter egos, The Plow Monsters, take the wheel. Fronted by the legendary Cowsick Steve and his bovine-defending anthems, The Plow Monsters prove that sometimes the best music comes from letting go of the steering wheel entirely.


We make music for misfits, thinkers, and anyone who's ever thought "yeah, but what if we tried THIS instead?"
